# travel.daveandmike.net

## Purpose
Dual-purpose travel site: active trip planning shared with co-travelers, and a post-trip archive of trip summaries.

## Author
- **Dave** (davidmusel) — sole builder and content author

## Audience
- During planning: Dave, Mike, and trip-specific co-travelers (informational — Dave publishes, others read)
- Post-trip: general visitors and future reference for Dave and Mike

## Travel Group
Dave and Mike travel regularly with others. Trips often include a consistent extended group — e.g., the upcoming Italy trip includes Mike's sister, Dave's sister and brother-in-law, and another couple (friends of Dave's sister and brother-in-law).

## Content Model
- **Active trip pages** — itineraries, logistics, and info for co-travelers to reference
- **Archive pages** — post-trip summaries and memories once a trip concludes

## Current State
- Design locked and consistent with dave&mike visual system
- Content structure: active planning and archive sections

## Always / Never
- Trip planning pages are informational, not collaborative — no suggestion forms, comment sections, or editable content
- Always consider that co-travelers will be reading on mobile — mobile layout matters
- Never mix active trip content with archived trips — keep them clearly separated
